Nubrikon is pleased to be a highly effective technology solution for problem-solving metalworking products. Nubrikon supplies not only the most technically innovative high temperature products, but also provides unmatched customer service and single-point accountability, all at a competitive price. This website is designed to help you learn about the many high tech lubricating products we offer.

Nubrikon focuses solely on the implementation and use of technology to meet the severe operating conditions found in the metal melting and metal forming industry today. Nubrikon is a leading provider of high temperature lubricants to all sections of commercial and manufacturing fields, providing many new and innovative forging lubricants used throughout the world.

Nubrikon’s forging lubricants for the ferrous and non-ferrous markets provide unequaled performance, with or without graphite. Our specialty greases for forging press gears, bearings, chains and cables give superior protection while being sensitive to the environment. The die casting release agents, ceramic cements and release coatings, and graphitic mold release coatings are also among the technology solutions provided by Nubrikon.
